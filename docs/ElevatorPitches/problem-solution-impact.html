Delivery Predictability

Problem

You're accountable for commitments you can't validate

Engineering leaders commit to delivery dates based on estimates from their teams. But they have no visibility into whether those estimates are reliable.

  • Blind trust: Plans are built on estimates taken at face value
  • No early warning: Problems surface when deadlines are missed, not before
  • Inconsistent inputs: Teams estimate differently, but it all gets rolled up the same way
  • Accountability gap: Leaders own the outcome but can't assess the inputs

Solution

Visibility into the reliability of your planning data

We analyze your Jira data to show you which estimates you can trust—before you commit to a plan built on them.

  • Estimation coverage: See what's being estimated vs. what's flying blind
  • Team reliability: Know which teams hit their estimates and which don't
  • Cross-team alignment: See where collaborating teams are out of sync
  • Risk indicators: Spot patterns that precede missed commitments

Impact

Commitments grounded in data you can see

When you commit to a date, you know what that commitment is built on. No more promises based on faith.

  • Fewer surprises: See risk before it becomes a missed deadline
  • Credible forecasts: Give dates you can actually defend
  • Targeted intervention: Know where to focus before it's too late
  • Stakeholder trust: Build a track record of keeping commitments

The One-Liner

We help engineering leaders see the reliability of their planning data—so they stop committing to plans that were never going to work.
