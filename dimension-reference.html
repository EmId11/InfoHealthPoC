Dimension 1

Invisible Work

"How does your team compare on invisible work indicators?"

workCaptured

Detects signs that work is happening outside of Jira - variability in throughput, inconsistent individual output, and unexplained stage time differences.

Decision Considerations
  • This dimension relies on statistical signals rather than direct measurement
  • May generate false positives if team has variable workloads by design
  • Most valuable for teams concerned about work visibility
Indicators (3)
Variability in throughput without corresponding variability in demand throughputVariability

Why it matters: If output swings wildly while incoming work stays steady, hidden work is likely absorbing team capacity unpredictably.
